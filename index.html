HELLO AND WELCOME

Pleroma Missions is an indigenous, Trans-Denominational Bible teaching and Missions Oriented ministry. The ministry is located in Nigeria with its Headquarters in Anyigba, Dekina Local Government Area of Kogi State. The Ministry came into being in 1988. God is presently preparing the grounds in all the earth for a stupendous deep-seated and far-reaching outpouring of His Spirit. As a result of this great outpouring of God's Spirit upon the earth, there shall be a massive in-gathering of souls into God's Kingdom known as the End-Time Harvest. Nigeria (amongst several other nations) shall be a Recipient of this coming great move. To this end, God has commissioned PLEROMA MISSIONS as a spiritual catalyst to: 1. Prepare the ground for revival 2. Help preserve gains of the coming Revival We believe strongly that in This day of God's power His people will be Willing to gather in the harvest of tribes and Nations.

OUR VISION

Our vision is To Teach and Disciple Believers & To mobilize and commission cross-cultural missionaries who will go into the unreached tribes of Nigeria, nations of Africa and beyond; Ministering a holistic gospel with the goal of raising mature, indigenous disciples and New Testament Churches.

OUR MINISTRY

Present Fields

Pleroma Missions is working as at now amongst the following tribes: Igala, Bassa Komo, Egbura, Agatu, Kupa, Kakanda and Ganagana Several others tribes are to be Engaged with time: The Eggan, Fulani, Buduma, Gerawa, and Shuwa Arab, amongst others.

Present Harvest Force

Presently, Pleroma Missions has a crop of men and women serving the Lord amongst the various Tribes listed above. These laborers are Committed to expanding the borders of God's Kingdom and make Christ known where He is yet to be clearly known. However many more are needed in order to engage other tribes and nations

KIDS SUPPORT

Missionary Kids Support Scheme is specifically aimed at raising funds to support missionary children’s general welfare and educational needs. We envisage that as God touches the hearts of brethren to give for this worthy and critical cause, missionaries will be able to face their primary calling of serving the LORD on their mission fields knowing that their children’s welfare and educational needs are taken care of.
